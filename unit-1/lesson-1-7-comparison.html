Unit 1 Capstone: Comparison

This is a skill topic — no new content, all synthesis. Use the six prior topics as your evidence base. The goal is to compare across regions and write like a historian.

Classroom Flow

1. Orient Yourself Review the learning targets, examine the Unit 1 map, and read the First & 10 narrative — it models cross-regional thinking before you do it yourself.
2. Build the Skill Study all six lecture cards on the Comparison skill, then complete the Comparison Matrix to organize your evidence by region and category.
3. Write and Reflect Practice writing with the AP Skill Builder and Primary Source Pair, then complete both Checkpoints — Checkpoint 2 is your capstone comparison essay.
